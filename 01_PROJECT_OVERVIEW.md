# WaveTap — Project Overview

**Wave. Tap. Book.**

## What is WaveTap?

WaveTap is a peer-to-peer platform that connects Deaf and Hard of Hearing individuals with sign language interpreters. No agencies. No middlemen. No gatekeepers.

The platform is a neutral aggregator. It facilitates the match between two parties and then steps aside. All agreements, invoicing, scheduling details, and payments happen off-platform, between the two people involved.

## The Story

In the Deaf world, everything starts with a wave. A wave across a room. A wave to say "I'm here." A wave that says "I see you." It's the most natural, most human way to begin — no sound needed, no barrier. Just one person making themselves visible to another.

Then comes the tap. A tap on the shoulder. The universal Deaf gesture that says "I need you." It's trust. It's an invitation. One person reaching out to another and saying — I have something ahead of me, and I'd like you beside me for it.

The signer waves. The interpreter taps in. The interpreter waves. The signer taps to confirm.

WaveTap is that moment between two people — the moment a need is seen and someone steps forward to meet it.

Wave. Tap. Book.

## Core Principles

1. **Neutral aggregator** — WaveTap connects. It does not employ, endorse, verify, or guarantee either party. The platform holds no liability for agreements made between users.
2. **P2P only** — No agencies, organisations, or institutional accounts. Individual humans on both sides.
3. **Off-platform everything** — Once a match is confirmed, contact details are exchanged and parties handle all further coordination (location specifics, invoicing, payment, cancellations) off-platform.
4. **Trust-based** — Interpreter credentials are self-declared. The signer decides who they are comfortable working with.
5. **Signer empowerment** — The Signer is the expert on their own needs. The platform does not suggest, prescribe, or gatekeep.
6. **Privacy-first** — Fuzzy locations, minimal data collection, full account deletion. No data lingers.
7. **Accessibility is foundational** — Not an afterthought. The primary user base is Deaf. Every decision flows from this.

## Launch Scope

- **Geography:** Australia only
- **Sign language:** Auslan (infrastructure supports multi-language expansion)
- **Platform:** Three surfaces, built together — a responsive **web app** (also home to the admin panel) and **native iOS & Android apps**. Web and native ship simultaneously from one monorepo and share a single design language (HeroUI). No PWA.
- **Funding context:** EAF/NDIS users are a core audience, but the platform does not integrate with, invoice for, or reference any funding scheme. That's between the parties.

## What WaveTap Is Not

- Not an interpreter agency or booking service
- Not a payment processor or invoicing system
- Not a verification or credentialing body
- Not a communication tool (no in-app chat, no video calling)
- Not a review or rating platform
- Not an employer of interpreters
